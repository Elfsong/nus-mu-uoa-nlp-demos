    Rohani

    This is a painting showing Rohani. The painting was made by Georgette Chen. Rohani was a student of Chen's at the Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts (NAFA). She was also a friend beyond the classroom. Here, she is vibrantly clothed in a matching red dress and headscarf. She is also wearing an accessory, a delicate gold pin in the shape of an R. Chen often drove Rohani home after school, as both lived around Siglap.
